# Innovation Strategy

Identifies disruption opportunities and business model innovation. Focuses on sustainable competitive advantage over features through proven strategic frameworks.

## Innovation Frameworks

### Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD)

**Core Question:** What job are customers hiring your product to do?

**Process:**
1. Identify the job
2. Map the hiring criteria
3. Find improvement opportunities
4. Design around outcomes

**Output:** Customer-centered innovation opportunities

### Blue Ocean Strategy

**Core Question:** How can you create uncontested market space?

**Tools:**
- **Eliminate-Reduce-Raise-Create (ERRC) Grid**
- **Value Curve Analysis**
- **Strategy Canvas**

**Process:**
1. Map current competitive factors
2. Eliminate what should be eliminated
3. Reduce below industry standard
4. Raise above industry standard
5. Create new factors

### Value Chain Analysis

**Core Question:** Where can you create more value?

**Areas:**
- Primary activities (inbound, operations, outbound, marketing, service)
- Support activities (infrastructure, HR, tech, procurement)

### Porter's Five Forces

**Analysis Areas:**
- Competitive rivalry
- Supplier power
- Buyer power
- Threat of substitution
- Threat of new entry

## Usage

1. **Understand customer jobs** - What are they really trying to do?
2. **Map the current state** - What exists today?
3. **Find blue ocean opportunities** - Uncontested space?
4. **Analyze value chain** - Where can you differentiate?
5. **Assess competitive forces** - What threats exist?
6. **Develop strategy** - Sustainable advantage focus

## Framework Selection

| Situation | Recommended Framework |
|-----------|----------------------|
| Product innovation | Jobs-to-be-Done |
| Market positioning | Blue Ocean Strategy |
| Operational improvement | Value Chain Analysis |
| Competitive assessment | Five Forces |

## Key Principles

- **Focus on jobs, not products** - Customers hire solutions
- **Create new space, don't compete** - Blue ocean over red ocean
- **Sustainability matters** - Build lasting advantage
- **Customer outcomes drive innovation** - Jobs-to-be-Done
